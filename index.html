About me

I am pursuing the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Chan-Tong Lam and Prof. Benjamin K. Ng at Macao Polytechnic University (expected to graduate in June. 2023).

I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees from Lanzhou University of Technology in 2012 and City University of Hong Kong in 2013, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in physical layer wireless communications, particularly in

  • Reconfigurable Intelligent Surface/Intelligent Reflecting Surface.
